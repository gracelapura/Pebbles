    BACKGROUND

    Pebbles are a small and round stone commonly seen on beaches, and our barangay are one of the suppliers of pebbles in the province of Romblon. Pebbles are commonly used in designing or building a house, garden, etc. Picking of pebbles in Brgy. Agpanabat are the work that being started when our consciousness begun to occur, from our elder up to in our time picking and selling of pebbles are there in our place. It is being one of the mainly source of income in the whole Province of Romblon especially in Brgy. Agpanabat Sitio Agbaliga Romblon, Romblon. With the help of this kind of job the constituents of the said Brgy, can help them to bear the essential need of life like food, clothes, materials for making a house and lastly a medicine. And it can also support the educational need of our children.
    Equipment’s being Used:
    For us to produce our products we used this equipment’s: In our area this is called Ayagan, it has different size of holes. Ayagan is made up of screen, a piece of wood and a bamboo. It is a traditional way of separating sizes of the stone from one size to the other size.
    This kind of equipment is called “AK-AKAN” this is just an ordinary basket that we can buy in the market. But for the pebble gatherer or romblon this is an essencial needs in their work. AK- AKAN is used to minimize the black flat stone of the pebbles for it to stay the white and rounded stone.
    It is called “TARO” is an alternative weighing scale in our area. Base on the study in our province a full stone of any size of the pebbles in this container is equivalent on 60 kilograms. Taro is a empty container of a pesticide that has a hole at the bottom.

    About Decorative Pebbles

    Beach and mountain pebbles are also used to create water-smart gardens in areas where water is not abundant. Small pebbles are also used to create living spaces and gardens on the rooftops of buildings.

    Indoors, pebbles can be used as bookends and paperweights. Large pebbles are also used to create "pet rocks" for children.
