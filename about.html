Decorative Pebbles

Pebbles is a small rounded stone originally get from the river, beach, and also under the soil. Pebbles is used in making and designing a house, landscaping, etc. In the province of Romblon specifically in Brgy. Agpanabat Sitio Agbaliga the only source of income is by picking and selling of pebbles. Beach Pebbles are got in the sea shore of the said place. The production of pebbles is in traditional way, the resident don’t used any machineries or equipment like spade etc., they only used their bare hand. Pebbles are being pic one by one if the sizes are big but if the sizes are small the resident used what they called “ayagan”. Ayagan is a made of plastic that being bought in the market. Ayagan have purpose and it is as follows:
1. To separate one size to the other size.
2. And the last one is to remove the small black flat stone.
After cleaning the stone, the members stocks all of the pebbles they’ve been collected and wait for a buyer to be sell.
